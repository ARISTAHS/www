ABOUT

'Detroit: Become Human' is set in 2038. Android robots are widely used all over the world, and the androids are very similar in appearance to humans. However, a robot is just a robot, and like humans, it has an ego and cannot think alone. From home Android to personal and public Android. They maintain a close relationship with humans and are merely objects that live together in this world. In this situation, 'Detroit: Become Human', which features three androids in different positions as the main characters, contains their stories and growth process.

If you are human and the androids you hire start a riot and take away valuables from you. Can you really trust Android? It may not be a good thing for a machine that has to live as a human assistant to have emotions. Because the unpredictable things that revolt against the creator, man, will become more likely to happen. 'Detroit: Become Human' makes you question yourself by putting such a negative aspect in the beginning. Will Android, which can become our future and reality, be a good thing or a bad thing for us? And as if to answer that question, the game ends in polar opposites depending on the player's choices. Maybe there is a negative ending about androids having an ego, or a positive ending.
